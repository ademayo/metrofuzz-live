About Metro Fuzz Radio

Metro Fuzz Radio is a non-commercial, non-profit, human-curated internet radio station by the Omaha Underground music community, for the Omaha Underground music community and its friends. We broadcast music, announcements, fake ads, and periodic call-in shows with Art Bell-esque late-night fever dream kinda flair.

Everything played on the station is curated by a passionate, opinionated group of fellow musicians. We aim to champion weird, original, and varied music made by real humans. We are always open to submissions, new ideas, fake ads, show announcements, and anything you can think of. We are entirely non-commercial.
